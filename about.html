About Us

About lingk

lingk is a free global IPTV streaming service designed to connect you with live television channels from every corner of the globe. Our mission is to make worldwide content accessible to everyone, breaking down geographical barriers and offering a view into different cultures, news, and entertainment. Dive into a vast and diverse library of channels, neatly organized into categories like Entertainment, News, Sports, Movies, Music, and more, all from the comfort of your browser.

Our Mission

At lingk, we value simplicity, reliability, and a seamless viewing experience. Our aim is to connect you with engaging content without clutter or broken links. We're here to spare you the hassle of endless searching, letting you simply tune in and enjoy.

Technology Behind the Scenes

lingk is made possible by powerful open-source tools. Our main video playback is powered by Shaka Player for a reliable streaming experience. The icons are provided by Google Fonts (Material Symbols), and our extensive channel list is sourced from the community-driven IPTV-org repository on GitHub.

Legal Disclaimer

lingk provides access to publicly available streams but does not host, own, or control any video content. We offer a simple way to explore and watch streams that we believe have been authorized for public distribution by the copyright holders. We do not guarantee the legality or quality of external content.
